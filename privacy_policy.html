Privacy Policy for QuickDone

Last Updated: May 15, 2024

iCradle Innovations ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by QuickDone.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application QuickDone, and its associated services (collectively, our "Service"). By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our application, including:

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you use our application, you may provide us with:

  • Task information and content you create, upload, or store in our application
  • Account information if you create an account (such as your name and email address)
  • Feedback and correspondence, such as information you provide when you respond to surveys, report a problem, or contact us

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our application, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information such as your mobile device ID, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system information
  • IP address and log information
  • App usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new products and features
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Understand how users use our application
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Protect against harmful or illegal activity

3. Permission Usage

3.1 Camera Permission

Our application requests access to your device's camera. This permission is used to:

  • Allow you to take photos to attach to your tasks
  • Scan QR codes or barcodes related to tasks (if applicable)

We do not access your camera without your knowledge, and all photos are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

3.2 Calendar Permission

Our application requests access to your device's calendar. This permission is used to:

  • Create calendar events based on your tasks and deadlines
  • Sync task due dates with your calendar
  • Send you reminders about upcoming tasks

We only access calendar information that is relevant to the tasks you create in our application.

3.3 Storage Permission

Our application requests access to your device's storage. This permission is used to:

  • Save task attachments (photos, documents, etc.) to your device
  • Access files you choose to attach to tasks
  • Cache application data to improve performance

We only access files that you explicitly select to be used with our application.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your consent to do so.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You can control certain information we collect by adjusting your device settings:

  • You can disable permissions such as camera, calendar, and storage access through your device settings
  • You can delete the application at any time, which will remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
